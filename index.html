Key principles
01
Start as early as possible
Early contributions have the longest compounding runway. Starting 5 years earlier can mean millions more at retirement.
02
Never stop investing
Keep contributing when markets fall -- you are buying at a discount. Consistency beats timing every time.
03
Invest every raise
Each time you get a pay rise, invest half and enjoy the other half. Your life improves and your retirement fund grows.
Conservative planning: underestimate returns (6-7%) - overestimate lifespan (plan to 90) - recalibrate every 5 years.
